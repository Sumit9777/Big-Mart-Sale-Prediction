Big Mart is a fictional retail store that operates in various locations. Predicting the sales of different products at Big Mart is crucial for optimizing inventory management and ensuring that the right products are available in the right quantities at the right time. Big Mart Sales Prediction is a data science and machine learning project aimed at building models that can accurately forecast the sales of various products based on historical data

Sales Of Products

The sales data in the context of Big Mart Sales Prediction typically refers to information about the quantity of products sold over a specific period. This data may include details such as product identifiers, store information, time periods (dates), and sales amounts. Analyzing this sales data can help identify patterns, trends, and factors influencing product sales. In the context of machine learning projects like Big Mart Sales Prediction, historical sales data is often used to train models that can predict future sales based on various features and parameters.

Analysis of Algorithms

Big Mart Sales Prediction aims to forecast product sales in Big Mart stores by leveraging historical data and machine learning. This involves collecting and cleaning datasets, exploring data patterns through visualization, and engineering features for model development. The choice of a regression model, such as Random Forest and K-Fold Cross Validation Technique, facilitates the training process. After evaluating model performance, hyperparameter tuning enhances accuracy.
